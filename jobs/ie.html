Industrial Engineers

The median salary for an Industrial Engineer is $96,320 according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics

If you get say, a 2% salary raise each year then after 1 year your salary would be $98,246.40

The second year your salary would be $100,211.33

The third year your salary would be $102,215.56

The fourth year your salary would be $104,259.87

And finally, after five years your salary would be $106,345.07
